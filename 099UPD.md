# Update 0.9.9 - Combat UI Overhaul & Shop Improvements

## Combat UI Redesign

### Visual Improvements
- Darkened combat background (55% darken) for better focus on gameplay
- Player character now has a styled panel frame (purple border with shadow, like shop)
- Player character made larger and positioned lower on screen
- Enemy sprite made smaller for better visual balance
- Turn banner now smaller and fades out completely instead of staying visible
- HP bars have cleaner styling with subtle borders
- Block labels and intent labels made more subtle
- HP labels inside bars now have outline for better readability

### Layout Changes
- Menu and Debug buttons moved to top left corner
- Energy panel positioned above Play Hand button (bottom left)
- Relics panel at top left (below menu buttons)
- Consumables panel moved next to Relics (top left area)
- Deck info simplified to minimal text labels ("Draw: X", "Discard: X")
- Removed unnecessary nested label nodes

### Popup Fixes
- Pause and Debug panels now have z_index=100 to render above cards
- Fixed null instance error with panel references

## Shop Improvements

### Pack Button Redesign
- UPG Pack and Card Pack icons now fill entire button
- Price displayed at bottom center with gold color and black outline (4px stroke)
- Prices are clearly visible and readable

### Scaling Prices
- All shop prices now scale with stage (+10% per stage):
  - UPG Pack: Base 60G
  - Card Pack: Base 50G
  - Upgrade Card: Base 30G

### Card Upgrade Cost
- Upgrade Card button now costs gold (was free)
- Cost scales with stage like other items

### UPG Pack Variety (Roguelike!)
- Now has 12 different options in the pool
- Randomly picks 4 options each time you open a pack
- New options added:
  - Vitality Orb (+8 Max HP)
  - Sharp Blade (+2 Attack damage permanently)
  - Iron Shield (+2 Block permanently)
  - Curse Scroll (Weaken next enemy)

### Full UPG Pack Option List
1. Health Potion - Heal 25 HP (storable)
2. Energy Drink - +2 Energy (storable)
3. Bomb - 15 Damage (storable)
4. Shield Scroll - +12 Block (storable)
5. Wisdom Scroll - Draw 3 cards (storable)
6. Lucky Coin - +30 Gold (storable)
7. Upgrade - Upgrade a card
8. Remove Card - Remove a card from deck
9. Vitality Orb - +8 Max HP
10. Sharp Blade - +2 Attack damage
11. Iron Shield - +2 Block
12. Curse Scroll - Weaken next enemy (storable)

## New Mechanics

### Next Enemy Weakened
- Curse Scroll applies weaken effect to the next enemy at combat start
- Effect is consumed after one combat

## Bug Fixes
- Fixed popup panels being covered by cards
- Fixed upgrade button allowing free upgrades
- Cleaned up unused nodes in combat scene
