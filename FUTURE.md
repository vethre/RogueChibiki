## Priority Suggestions

Based on game balance and player engagement, here's my suggested priority:

### High Priority
1. Relics/Artifacts - Adds depth without major rework
2. Card Upgrades - Natural progression system
3. Enemy Variety - Prevents repetitive gameplay
4. Events - Breaks up combat pacing

### Medium Priority
5. Achievements - Gives goals to work toward
6. Status Effects - More strategic combat
7. Run History - Player requested feature often
8. Multiple Bosses - More memorable encounters

### Lower Priority (but cool)
9. Daily Challenges - Needs leaderboard infrastructure
10. Multiplayer - Significant development effort
11. Card Crafting - Complex balancing required
12. Ascension Mode - Needs base game polish first
