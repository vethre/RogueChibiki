NEW FEATURES FOR v0.9.5

Relics/Artifacts
- Passive items collected during runs
- Examples:
  - "Lucky Coin" - +10% gold from all sources
  - "Thick Skin" - Start each combat with 3 block
  - "Energy Drink" - +1 energy on first turn only
- Obtained from: Elite rewards, boss rewards, shops, events

Card Upgrades
- Upgrade cards at rest sites or shops
- Upgraded cards have enhanced effects:
  - Strike → Strike+ (7 damage → 10 damage)
  - Defend → Defend+ (5 block → 8 block)
- Visual indicator for upgraded cards

Events (Random Encounters)
- Non-combat encounters between fights
- Examples:
  - "Mysterious Stranger" - Trade HP for gold or card
  - "Abandoned Chest" - Risk trap for reward
  - "Training Dummy" - Upgrade a card for free
  - "Shady Merchant" - Cheap prices but cursed cards

New Cards
- **Character-Specific Cards** - Unique cards only available to certain characters
- **Rare Cards** - Powerful cards with low drop rates in shops/rewards
- **Curse Cards** - Negative cards added to deck as penalties (from events/elites)

Skins
- Spring Collection (Main Menu new pop-up banner w/ skins): Spring Chriswave, Spring Gaechka & Spring Dangerlyoha
- Buy skins with new valute: Bouquets
- Bouquets can be obtained while finishing finite run, and every infinite 10th stage
